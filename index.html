About Game

You have to attend your interview at RBS in 5 minutes and you are in need of a suit worth 100 dollars.
You can use debit card,cash,cheque etc.
If found doing any kind of fraud , you would be caught by the police.
Once you reach your interview (when it starts in 5 minutes) the game will end.
Debit card will be cashed in after 2 minutes, cheque after 3 minutes by the merchant.
Navigate around the locality to find new ways to earn and exchange money.

About Us

This project is an overview on the fraudulent practices that can be carried out in online transactions. This helps us to build more secure products in future.
